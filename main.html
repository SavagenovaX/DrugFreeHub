⚠ Admin Access Rules

• Super Admins have full access and can add, edit, revoke, and delete any admin account.
• Regular Admins can add new admins but cannot modify or revoke Super Admin accounts.
• An admin cannot revoke their own account.
• Revoked admins lose all access immediately but their account record is retained.

Resilience & Coping

Resilience is the capability to bounce back from stress or disappointment in healthy ways without turning to substances.

Assertiveness Strategies

Assertiveness means projecting clear personal decisions respectfully. Practice saying "No, thank you. That is not my choice." calmly.

Decision Making Framework

Every choice carries a consequence. Identify the problem, explore consequences, then act. Seek trusted adult guidance whenever in doubt.
